About the club

A new club, built on spirit and speed.

Ultimate Frisbee blends the flow of soccer, the cuts of basketball, and the catches of football — all without referees. The game runs on Spirit of the Game, where players call their own fouls and respect comes first.

tlvulti is just getting started, and that's the best time to join. Beginners are genuinely welcome — come learn to throw, run, and play alongside people figuring it out right beside you.
