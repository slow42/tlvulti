About the club

New club, established community

The club

tlvulti plays 4v4 Ultimate Frisbee — fast, self-refereed, and open to everyone. The game runs on Spirit of the Game, where players call their own fouls and respect comes before competition.

No experience needed — we'll teach you to throw, cut, and play from your very first session.

How to get involved

The community

Behind the new name is a real Tel Aviv community — players who train together weekly, welcome newcomers, and make the field feel like home from day one.

Our coaching staff brings years of experience, running structured sessions for every level from first-timers to competitive players.
